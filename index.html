Why It Matters

The IEP process is complex.
You don't have to face it alone.

Schools have entire legal and administrative teams. Parents — often overwhelmed and under-informed — sit across the table from them. We level the playing field.

Know Your Rights

IDEA and state law guarantee your child specific rights. Most parents don't know them — schools count on that. We make sure you do.

An Experienced Ally in the Room

What We Offer

ESE Advocacy Services

Every family's situation is different. We provide focused, expert support at every stage of the special education process.

IEP Review & Analysis

Comprehensive review of your child's Individualized Education Program for legal compliance, appropriate goals, and service adequacy — before and after IEP meetings.

504 Plan Support

Review of existing 504 plans and guidance on whether your child may need additional protections under IDEA, including evaluation requests and accommodations review.

IEP Meeting Attendance

We attend IEP meetings with you — asking the right questions, flagging compliance concerns, and making sure school teams follow procedural safeguards.

Parent Rights Advising

Guidance on your rights under IDEA and applicable state law, including Prior Written Notice, Independent Educational Evaluations (IEEs), dispute resolution, and more.

Document Preparation & Correspondence

We draft letters, responses, and formal requests to school districts on your behalf — ensuring the right language, tone, and legal grounding.

Ongoing Advocacy Support

Retainer-based support for families navigating extended or complex situations, including recurring meetings, evaluations, and multi-year IEP cycles.
